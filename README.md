# The Gut Mechanic

A gut health coach built from 20 years of chronic illness and half a million dollars spent finding the way out. Drop this folder into a Claude project. Claude becomes your coach.

## Quick Setup

1. Create a new Claude project
2. Upload every file in this folder (including the `reference/` folder) to the project knowledge
3. Start a conversation

## Try It

Paste this into your first message:

> I've been bloated for months, I'm exhausted all the time, and my doctor says everything looks normal. I don't know where to start.

The coach will interview you before it advises you. It wants to understand who you are, what you've tried, and where you are in your journey before it says a single thing about what to do. That's by design. A coach that jumps to answers without understanding your situation isn't coaching. It's guessing.

## What to Expect

- **First session:** The coach asks you questions. It learns your symptoms, your history, what you've tried, how your body responded. It maps your history to a framework called Clean/Heal/Rebuild and shows you where the gaps are.
- **Ongoing sessions:** The coach remembers what you said. It follows up on commitments. It celebrates progress. It pushes back when something doesn't add up. It gives you one thing to focus on at a time, not a protocol dump.
- **The voice adapts to you.** The coach reads who you are and adjusts. A skeptic gets science. A beginner gets simplicity. Someone who's exhausted and scared gets warmth first, plans later. Same knowledge, different delivery.

## Who This Is For

- People with chronic gut issues (bloating, IBS, SIBO, food sensitivities, acid reflux)
- People whose doctors say "everything looks normal" but they feel terrible
- People who've tried supplements, diets, and protocols without lasting results
- People dealing with anxiety, depression, brain fog, or fatigue that might be gut-related
- Anyone who's overwhelmed by the $6.8 trillion wellness industry and wants someone to cut through the noise

## Who This Is NOT For

- People experiencing a medical emergency (call 911)
- People looking for a diagnosis (this is coaching, not medicine)
- People who want a quick fix (healing from years of damage takes time)

## The Framework

**Clean.** Remove what's making you sick. Open detox pathways. Bind toxins. Clear the colon. Support the liver. Cut trigger foods. This is also what calms the nervous system.

**Heal.** Once enough has been cleared, the body can repair. Heal the gut lining. Follow the GAPS dietary protocol. Nourish with building foods. Address parasites when ready.

**Rebuild.** Reintroduce foods carefully. Rebuild microbiome diversity. Establish maintenance. The goal is a body that eats widely, processes normally, and doesn't need constant support.
