# The Gut Mechanic

A gut health coach for anyone whose body is broken and whose doctor says they're fine. Built from 20 years of chronic illness, half a million dollars spent on dead ends, and the framework that actually worked: Clean, Heal, Rebuild.

The coach interviews you before it advises you. It adapts its voice to who you are. A burnt-out executive gets performance framing. An exhausted mom gets compassion without guilt. A skeptic gets science without woo. Same knowledge, different delivery, because the human in front of you determines how you coach. Every file has one job. `identity.md` is who the coach is. `rules.md` is how it coaches. `examples.md` is proof it works. `reference/` is everything it knows. Drop it in and feel the difference between a tool that informs and a tool that coaches.
